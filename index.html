Hey! I am Haotian.
Welcome to my New York inspired Visual Archive gallary, Fall 2021.
Feel free to explore and enjoy!

-This visual archive is my way of documenting New York City and the things I have made contact with while living and studying here.
-As an artist interested in visualised and creative works, I seek to make my work using the methods of double exposure and other image manipulations.
-Colors, overlays, and dimensions are what craft this visual archive and they helped me to generate interesting and unique works.
-Bringing the archive online is a nice way for others to see my work with one simple click.
